-   Specificity Hirerachy:

        There are 4 categories which define the Specificity level of the Selectors.

        1. Inline styles:
            Eg: <h1 style='color: pink;'>

        2. Ids:
            Eg: #navbar

        3. Classes, pseudo-classes, attribute-selectors:
            Eg: .test, :hover, [href]

        4. Elements and pseudo-elements
            Eg: h1, ::before
